My name is Edson da Silva. I was born and raised in the state of Mato Grosso, located in the northwest part of Brazil. I started working very early in life in different jobs requiring physical labor and was not exposed to any type of technology until later in life. I found myself wanting more in life, in 2007 I came to Florida in order to pay for my brother’s education. I worked at Fogo de Chão, a Brazilian steakhouse for over eight years. During these years, I learned many aspects of business administration and was also exposed to many different ways of using technology. I thrived and enjoyed the service industry, even became part owner of an Italian restaurant; though I always knew that it was only a stepping stone for me to achieve other goals. After speaking to Mr. Jones about the endless possibilities around coding, I decided that it would be the perfect opportunity for me. I plan to master coding while creating a successful career, and one day hope to help many people in the United States as well as in my home country of Brazil.
